The Schools of Montesca and Rovigliano
at the Universal Exposition of 1910

In 1910, the rural schools of Montesca and Rovigliano, founded by Alice Hallgarten-Franchetti, presented their innovative educational work at the Universal and International Exposition in Brussels.

The 2024 exhibition “Alice in Brussels” celebrates their award-winning, progressive model of rural education.
